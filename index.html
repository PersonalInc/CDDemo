Satyendar Kumar Jain, is a Cabinet Minister in the Government of Delhi and is a politician from Aam Aadmi Party

He was one of the seven Cabinet Ministers in the Government of Delhi led by Arvind Kejriwal.[2] His portfolio includes Health, Industries, Home, Public Work Department, Power.[2] He is currently in his second term as Delhi Health Minister, having previously served as Health and Industries Minister from 28 December 2013 to 14 February 2014 in the previous AAP Government.Satyendar Jain is an architect by profession..
